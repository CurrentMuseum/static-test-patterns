The Current

The Current’s collection is focused on the impact of technology on the human condition.

We seek works that are digitally-native, making the actual artwork itself accessible among our supporters. The Current's collection is your collection as well.

Conditions for Acquisition

  • The Artist is living
  • The Studio is open to working with The Current on our Artist Lifetime Royalties Contract for Acquisition
  • The work can be exhibited in two form factors: screen-based and installation-based formats
  • The work offers a critical inquiry into technology’s impact on humans

Guiding Principles

  • Aesthetically pleasing
  • Pushes genres forward/together
  • Has a rich back story and/or public reaction
  • Aiming to represent a broad range of artistic practice:
    • Pioneers (20+ years working in New Media)
    • Emerging Market Artists (Mid-market, Institutional Shows)
    • Emerging Artists (Early career)
