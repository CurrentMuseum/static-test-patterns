The Current // Permanence
Hosted by Ben Fino-Radin

Artworks selected by Ben Fino-Radin are presented salon-style for members of The Current in our SoHo location. Join us for a discussion on the topic of 'Permanence' on Wednesday, May 23rd at 8PM EST.

Join our Cooperative Collection Committee

Our cooperative collections committee is open to anyone, you can register online. Committee members attend our salons by invitiation – they receive details on the works we are considering for The Current’s collection, and have a vote on which works from the salon should be added to the collection. Our committee members also have special access to enjoy the artworks in the museum’s collection.

Committee Member – $250/quarter

Vote on artworks for the collection, and attend the salons in-person

Patron – $500/quarter

Special artist events, vote on artworks for the collection, and attend the salons in-person +1

We are a non-profit cooperative collection of contemporary media art. Our mission is to experiment with the role of museums through exhibition, support, and preservation of artistic works that engage technology.
