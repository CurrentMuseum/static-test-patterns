The Current

We are a non-profit cooperative collection of contemporary media art.

Our mission is to experiment with the role of museums through exhibition, support, and preservation of artistic works that engage technology.

Our first two salons, were on the topics of "Identity", and "Permanence" and our next, "Truth", will be on September 14th, 2018 at 8pm. Join our Collection Committee to attend in-person and vote on what we collect.
