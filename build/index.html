The Current

We are a non-profit institution devoted to electronic media.

Our mission is to create experiences that showcase, study, and preserve the works of digitally-bold artists who engage technology to expand the boundaries of creative expression.

This Fall, The Current is inviting artists, technologists, scholars and the art-curious to participate in a battery of tests concerning the future of museums and digital art.

  • Nov 4, 2016 5–8pm $25 Tickets

    Opening: The Current Test Patterns and TRANSFER Download

    Transfer Gallery experiments with digital media formats by showcasing rotating immersive 3D video, generative software, and animation works by artists including Philip Stearns and Rosa Menken.

  • Nov 10, 2016 5–8pm $25 Tickets

    Premiere of Moraine Shoal

    Avant and The Current, present a site-specific sound environment and software-score by artist-composer Seth Cluett.

  • Nov 17, 2016 5–8pm $25 Tickets

    The Current Hypothesis

    An intimate cross-industry roundtable to critique, brainstorm, and discuss the future of museums in a digital age. We’ll ask the hard questions and test our thesis from conservation to commerce, aesthetics to accessibility, with a goal of informing our next steps as an institution.

  • Oct 25, 2016 5–8pm $25 Tickets
    Nov 29, 2016 5–8pm

    Code + Craft

    Think drink ‘n draw, but no nude models; just naked algorithms. For artists, coders, and tinkerers to chill and create experimental art. Bring your laptops, we’ll bring hot-glue.

  • Dec 7, 2016 5–8pm $25 Tickets

    This is Test. This is Only a Test.

    Artists, art historians and media theorists lead a discussion about Test Patterns facilitated by The Current curator Kelly Rae Aldridge.

  • Dec 10, 2016 5–8pm $25 Tickets

    Art Concert

    An experiment in art engagement with tunes by DJ ËGÅ, Art by Kawandeep Virdee, Rosa Menken, and Virtual Reality experiences by Cabbibo.
